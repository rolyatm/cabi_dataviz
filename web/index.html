Capital Bikeshare

Analysis of 2012 ridership

Washington, D.C.'s Capital Bikeshare program provides easy access to bikes in DC. In 2012, over 2 million rides were taken on the system.

This visualization is an analysis of how riders travel through DC. Viewing a year's worth ride data allows us to see the movement patterns through the city and identify heavily used biking corridors.

Click on a station to see routes between other stations.
SHIFT + click on a second station to see details about that specific route.
